Getting Here

Arrive Easily

Nearest station: Liverpool Lime Street - 0.9 miles

Local buses connect to Liverpool city centre, stopping near Chapel Street.

From M62:

Exit at Junction 4 (Liverpool).

Follow A5088 towards Liverpool city centre.

Turn onto Chapel Street; the hotel is near the Royal Liver Building.

8 miles to Liverpool John Lennon Airport.

Pet-Friendly

Welcome Four-Legged Guests

Our dog-friendly rooms come with treats, bowls, and access to Liverpool’s waterfront walks. Dogs must be on leads in public areas and supervised (max two dogs under 15kg, £20/night per pet, service dogs free).
