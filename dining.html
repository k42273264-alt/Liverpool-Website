Vista Restaurant & Vu Bar & Terrace

Vista Restaurant & Vu Bar & Terrace, our signature dining venue, offers an elegant setting with seasonal menus showcasing locally sourced ingredients, perfect for special occasions. Located on the upper floors, it accommodates up to 120 guests with breathtaking views of Liverpool's waterfront.
