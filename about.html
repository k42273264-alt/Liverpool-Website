Our Story

Liverpool Atlantic Tower Hotel by Sunday

1972: Maritime Heritage

Opened in 1972, the Liverpool Atlantic Tower Hotel by Sunday stands as a 4-star landmark on Chapel Street, designed to reflect Liverpool's maritime history with its ship-prow architecture near the Royal Liver Building.

Modern Luxury Today

Today, the hotel blends its maritime heritage with contemporary elegance, offering 225 spacious rooms, fine dining at Vu Bar & Terrace, and versatile event spaces with stunning Mersey views.
